Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 6, 2025

Tech In Box ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you visit our website, use our services, or interact with our digital marketing campaigns. We are located at 10 No, Puliya Square, Satpratap Complex, Nagpur 440014, India.

1

Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

You may share information with us when you:

  • Fill out a contact or consultation form on our website
  • Request a quote or proposal for our services
  • Subscribe to updates or newsletters
  • Communicate with us via email, phone, WhatsApp, or social media
  • Engage our services for SEO, digital marketing, web/app development, graphic design, content creation, Meta Ads, or PR releases

This information may include: Name, business name, job title, email address, phone number, website URL, business details, project requirements, budget, objectives, and any other information you voluntarily submit.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website or landing pages, we automatically collect certain data including:

  • IP address and approximate location
  • Browser type and device information
  • Pages viewed, time spent, and navigation patterns
  • Referring websites or advertisements that led you to us

This information is collected through analytics tools and cookies to improve performance and user experience.

2

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for purposes such as:

  • Responding to your inquiries, consultation requests, and service queries
  • Evaluating your business needs and preparing proposals or digital strategies
  • Delivering and managing SEO, digital marketing, web development, app development, graphic design, content creation, Meta Ads, and PR services
  • Sending service-related updates, campaign reports, and performance summaries
  • Improving our website, content, and service offerings
  • Running advertising and remarketing campaigns (where applicable)
  • Fulfilling legal, regulatory, and contractual obligations

We focus on using your data to support measurable results—such as leads, conversions, ROI, and revenue growth—not vanity metrics.

3

Cookies, Analytics, and Advertising

Our website and service pages may use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences (forms, language settings)
  • Analyze website traffic and user behavior
  • Measure campaign performance and engagement
  • Support remarketing or retargeting on platforms such as Meta (Facebook & Instagram) and Google

You can modify your browser settings to block or limit cookies. However, some website features may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

4

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information only in limited situations:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party vendors (hosting, analytics, email tools, advertising platforms) who assist us in delivering services
  • Payment Partners: With payment or invoicing partners where applicable
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, regulation, or legal proceedings
  • Rights Protection: To protect our rights, safety, or the rights and safety of others
  • Business Transfers: As part of a business transfer, merger, or restructuring

All third parties who process data on our behalf are expected to handle it securely and only for the agreed purpose.

5

Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including:

  • Using secure connections (where implemented) for data transmission
  • Restricting access to business systems to authorized personnel only
  • Following best practices around passwords, devices, and account security
  • Regular security monitoring and updates

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide services and support your ongoing projects
  • Comply with legal, tax, and regulatory requirements
  • Maintain records of communication, proposals, and contracts

If you no longer wish us to keep your information (subject to legal requirements), you may request deletion as described below.

7

Your Rights and Choices

Depending on applicable laws, including India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023, you may have the right to:

  • Access: Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data, where legally permissible
  • Opt-out: Object to or restrict certain types of processing (such as marketing emails)
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent

Tech In Box primarily provides services to businesses and professionals, including startups, SMEs, and enterprises. While individuals may contact us for services, our solutions, reporting, and data use are generally designed for business contexts and marketing activities.
